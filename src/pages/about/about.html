Crash Area Alert

Area Crash Alert is a notification application to road users that enable all users to be notified of the dangers on the road they are going through. This application will help road users to be more aware of road conditions will be passed by them. The users will be notified that the area to be traversed is often the area of ​​the accident so that they are more cautious in addition to this application will help users to be more concerned with the environment.
